Ramin.

You meet Ramin, a Software Engineer with over 7 years of experience in web development. He enjoys building everything from clean user interfaces to solid backend systems, and he’s now learning more about Data and Machine Learning.
He spent 3 years at Digikala Group, one of the biggest online stores in the Middle East, working on products used by 10+ million people every day and 40+ million active users.
Later, he joined Boozt, the largest online store in the Nordics, helping make shopping and CMS fast, modern, and easy for people.
Now he lives in Sweden and works at Sinch, a global leader in cloud communications. Sinch powers billions of messages, calls, and verifications for companies like Google, Uber, and PayPal.
At Sinch, Ramin helps build safe, reliable, and creative tools that keep people and businesses connected every day.
